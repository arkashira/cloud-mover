- **Zero‑code configuration** – YAML‑based declarative manifests let developers stay in their existing CI/CD pipelines.
- **Compliance‑ready audit trail** – immutable logs satisfy GDPR, PCI‑DSS, and local Thai data‑sovereignty regulations.
